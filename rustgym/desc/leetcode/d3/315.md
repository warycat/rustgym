<div><p>You are given an integer array <i>nums</i> and you have to return a new <i>counts</i> array. The <i>counts</i> array has the property where <code>counts[i]</code> is the number of smaller elements to the right of <code>nums[i]</code>.</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Example 1:</strong></p>

<pre><strong>Input:</strong> nums = [5,2,6,1]
<strong>Output:</strong> [2,1,1,0]
<strong>Explanation:</strong>
To the right of 5 there are <b>2</b> smaller elements (2 and 1).
To the right of 2 there is only <b>1</b> smaller element (1).
To the right of 6 there is <b>1</b> smaller element (1).
To the right of 1 there is <b>0</b> smaller element.
</pre>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Constraints:</strong></p>

<ul>
	<li><code>0 &lt;= nums.length &lt;= 10^5</code></li>
	<li><code>-10^4&nbsp;&lt;= nums[i] &lt;= 10^4</code></li>
</ul>
</div>
